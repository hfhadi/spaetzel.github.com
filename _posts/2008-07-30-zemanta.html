I just installed the Zemanta plugin for Firefox. It is a cool little plugin that suggests a number of articles, tags and photos to go along with the blog post that you are currently typing. This entry exists purely for me to test out its functionality. I'm assuming that it will do a pretty good job of finding posts that are related to itself.
